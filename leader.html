Our Leadership

Natraj Nukala

Natraj Nukala

He has an undergraduate degree in engineering from India and a master's in risk management from New York University, USA. He has been involved with the insurance sector for over a decade while his family interests include agriculture & textile businesses. This gave him a first-hand experience and deep insight into the Indian agri sector and the workings of the Indian farmer. He decided to bring his expertise and his experience together to create Kshema General Insurance Ltd. He also established a successful insurance broking company, ITUS before taking the plunge in crop insurance with Kshema.

Dr Vyasa Krishna Burugupalli

Dr Vyasa Krishna Burugupalli

Dr Burugupalli is a veteran of the insurance industry with an illustrious career spanning over 45 years in different regulatory regimes across continents. He was the Chief Executive Officer of the First Insurance Company Limited in Kampala before taking over as the MD and CEO of Kshema General Insurance Limited. His leadership also extends to guiding various NGOs, including the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation grantees, on microinsurance. He holds a doctoral qualification in insurance risk management. He is a Fellow of the Australian and New Zealand Institute of Insurance and Finance as well as a Certified Insurance Practitioner. He is also a Fellow of the Insurance Training College of Uganda.

Rajeshnani Dasari

Rajeshnani Dasari

Rajesh is a technologist with over two decades of experience across insurance, automotive, manufacturing, pharmaceutical and high-tech industries. He was with Oracle Corporation as Senior Director in emerging technologies of Mobility, Bots, IoT, and Blockchain in his most recent stint. Prior to this, he was leading Oracle’s efforts to serve customers in their adoption of cloud and emerging technologies. Rajesh is an alumnus of prestigious institutions like BITS Pilani (India), Clemson University, South Carolina (USA), and State University of New York College at Buffalo (USA).

Deepa Karthykeyan

Deepa is co-founder and partner of Athena Infonomics – a global impact solutions group. Deepa has led several programmes in Asia and Africa with a focus on advancing equitable and safe public services in changing urban contexts. She serves as part of the Technical Advisory Group for the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development Data (GPSDD) and is a member of the IWA digital water program steering committee. Deepa holds a master’s degree in economics from the National University of Singapore.
